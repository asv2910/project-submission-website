About Sweet Haven

Where tradition meets innovation

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sweet Haven Bakery began with a simple mission: to bring authentic, artisan-quality baked goods to our community. What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved local destination.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, traditional baking techniques, and a whole lot of love in everything we create. From our signature sourdough to custom celebration cakes, every item tells a story of craftsmanship and care.

Our master bakers arrive before dawn to ensure you get the freshest bread and pastries. We source locally whenever possible and never compromise on quality.
